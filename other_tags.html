Give text" most commonly refers to Text-to-Give, a convenient fundraising method where people text a keyword and amount (e.g., "GIVE 20") to a specific number to donate instantly from their phones, with the money processed and recorded for nonprofits. It also can mean adding text to images (like in Canva) or sending a simple text message. For donations, it's a quick way for supporters to contribute via SMS, often linking to a secure payment form for first-time donors to set up their details.
